# LinkMAX — Sales OS for Service Businesses

> The fastest way for a small service business to turn a link into a paying customer.

---

## 1. Problem

Small service businesses (beauty, fitness, tutoring, agencies, freelancers) lose **~70% of inbound leads** because their digital presence is fragmented:

- **Linktree-style pages** — pretty, but no sales funnel.
- **Instagram bio link** — one URL, zero tracking.
- **Full CRMs** (HubSpot, Bitrix) — too complex, too expensive, abandoned in 2 weeks.
- **Generic site builders** — beautiful sites, but no leads, no booking, no payment.

Result: messages get lost in DMs, bookings happen by manual back-and-forth, and revenue leaks.

---

## 2. Solution — Sales OS

**LinkMAX is a Sales Operating System** purpose-built for solo & small-team service businesses. One link replaces:

| Replaces | Cost saved |

**One subscription. One workflow. Lead → Booking → Payment → Repeat.**
