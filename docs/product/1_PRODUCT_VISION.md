# 1. Product Vision & Strategy

> **Status:** Active
> **Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**lnkmx** is the **Micro-Business Operating System**. We are not just another link-in-bio tool; we are a verticalized platform that enables solopreneurs and micro-businesses to **Build** their presence, **Manage** their clients, and **Grow** their revenue—all from a single mobile-first dashboard.

Our core thesis: **The "Tool Tax" is killing micro-businesses.** They shouldn't need Wix for a site, Calendly for bookings, Mailchimp for leads, and Google Sheets for CRM. lnkmx consolidates this stack into one 15-minute setup.

---

## 2. Mission & Vision

### Mission
To democratize professional business infrastructure for the next generation of solopreneurs, enabling them to look like Fortune 500 companies without the cost or complexity.

### Vision
To become the default "Identity + Business OS" for every service provider in Central Asia and emerging markets, where every "link in bio" is actually a fully functioning business backend.

### Core Values
1.  **Simplicity as a Religion**: If it takes more than 3 clicks, it's broken. We target users who are experts in *baking/teaching/selling*, not IT.
2.  **Premium by Default**: Our "Liquid Glass" design system ensures that even a free user looks expensive. Aesthetics is our viral loop.
3.  **Native Power**: We don't just "link out" to other tools; we *replace* them. Native forms, native bookings, native analytics.

---

## 3. The "Why Now?" (Market Timing)
1.  **Creator -> Founder Shift**: The creator economy is maturing. Creators are realizing vanity metrics (likes) don't pay bills—clients and sales do. They need business tools.
2.  **Verticalization of Software**: General purpose website builders (Wix) are too broad. Specialized, vertical tools for "Bio Commerce" are the next wave.
3.  **Mobile-First Management**: 90% of our users run their entire business from a smartphone. Desktop-centric tools like Salesforce or WordPress are obsolete for them.

## 5. Strategic Pillars (Our Bets)

### Bet 1: The "Liquid Glass" Aesthetic (Design as a Moat)
Most bio-link tools look cheap and flat. We invested heavily in a proprietary design system—**Liquid Glass**—featuring glassmorphism, rich motion, and depth.
*   **Result**: Users switch to us purely for the "look", then stay for the features.

### Bet 2: Vertical Integration (The "All-in-One" Wedge)
We don't want to integrate with Calendly; we want to kill Calendly for this segment. By owning the data (Lead -> CRM -> Booking -> Payment), we build a data moat that simple link aggregators cannot match.

### Bet 3: Hyper-Localization (Regional Dominance First)
Global competitors (Linktree) ignore local nuances. We win by integrating local payment gateways (Kaspi, Robokassa), local languages (KZ/RU/EN), and local auth methods (Telegram integration).

### Positioning vs Bitrix24 and heavy CRMs
We do **not** compete with Bitrix24 or enterprise CRMs on breadth. We position lnkmx as the **anti-Bitrix for solopreneurs**: simple, fast, mobile-first, and working out of the box in 15 minutes—no implementation, no programmers. Where Bitrix24 is complex, slow, desktop-centric, and expensive per seat, lnkmx is «3 clicks», lightweight analytics, PWA + native apps, and a single Pro tariff. We deliver *just enough* CRM (deals, contacts, tasks, automations, invoices) to run a one-person or micro team, and deliberately avoid BPMN, telephony, HR, and document workflow.

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **North Star**: **Active Micro-Businesses** (Users with >1 lead/booking per month).
*   **Acquisition**: Viral Coefficient (K-factor) from "Powered by lnkmx" watermark.
*   **Retention**: % of users who log in weekly to check CRM/Analytics.
*   **Monetization**: Conversion rate from Free -> Pro (target 3-5%).
