# 3. Marketing & Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

> **Status:** Active
> **Last Updated:** February 28, 2026
> **Focus:** Growth & User Acquisition

---

## 1. The Strategy: "Product-Led Local Domination"
Our strategy relies on **Product-Led Growth (PLG)** fueled by a viral loop, reinforced by **hyper-local** marketing campaigns. We do not compete on "Better Features" alone; we compete on "Better Business Results" for specific niches.

---

## 2. Acquisition Channels

### 2.1. The Viral Loop (The Engine)
*   **Mechanism**: Every Free page has a sticky "Powered by lnkmx" badge.
*   **User Flow**: Visitor lands on a Creator's page -> Sees beautiful design -> Clicks "Powered by" -> Signs up.
*   **Optimization**: We will A/B test the badge copy ("Get your own page" vs "Create free website").

### 2.2. Niche Domination (Outbound/Content)
We are targeting **3 Beachhead Markets** to start.

**Content & comparison:** Comparison page with lnkmx vs Linktree, Taplink, Carrd, Beacons and «Почему не Bitrix24 для соло»: [/alternatives](https://lnkmx.my/alternatives). Use in campaigns and SEO.

#### A. Beauty Masters (Salons, Lash Techs, Barbers)
*   **Pain**: "DM me for appointment" is chaotic. No deposit system.
*   **Offer**: "Get a booking link in 5 mins."
*   **Channel**: Instagram/TikTok DMs, partnerships with local beauty schools.

#### B. Independent Realtors
*   **Pain**: Generic agency pages don't capture personal leads.
*   **Offer**: "Your personal portfolio with lead capture."
*   **Channel**: Local real estate agency partnerships.

#### C. Educational Coaches/Mentors
*   **Pain**: Selling webinars/courses via card-to-card transfers.
*   **Offer**: "Sell tickets and automate access."
*   **Channel**: Industry conferences, webinar sponsorships.

## 3. Brand Identity
**"The Apple of Solopreneur Tools"**

*   **Voice**: Empowering, Professional, Concise. We don't use tech jargon (no "DNS", "API", "Integration"). We use business terms ("Growth", "Clients", "Sales").
*   **Visuals**: **Liquid Glass**. High-end, premium, trustworthy. When a user sees a lnkmx page, it should signal "This professional is successful."

---

## 4. Pricing Strategy

### Philosophy
**"Come for the Link, Stay for the Business."**
We use a **Freemium** model. The Free tier is generous enough to go viral, but the Pro tier unlocks "Making Money" features.

### Tiers

| Tier | Price (KZT) | Price (USD) | value Proposition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | 0 ₸ | $0 | **Identity**. Enough to exist online. 1 Page, standard blocks. Includes Watermark. |
| **Pro** | 2,900 ₸/mo | ~$6.50 | **Business**. Remove watermark, capture leads, accept bookings, advanced analytics. |
| **Agency** | Call us | Custom | **Scale**. Managing 10+ profiles (for agencies/brands). *Planned.* |

### Psychology
*   **2,900 KZT** is the price of ~2 cups of coffee. It is a "no-brainer" impulse buy for a business tool.
*   **Annual Discount**: 2 months free (29,000 KZT/year) to drive cash flow.
