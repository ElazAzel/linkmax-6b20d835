## What is LinkMAX?

## FAQ — Common Questions

### How much does LinkMAX cost?
Three tiers:
1. **Identity (free forever)** — 1 page, 11 blocks, lnkmx.my/username subdomain
2. **Starter (Success)** — $0/mo + 7% per successful sale. Full CRM + payments
3. **Pro (Business OS)** — ~$7/mo. 6 pages, custom domains, white-label, 0–1% fee

### How is LinkMAX different from Linktree/Taplink?
LinkMAX is not just a "link-in-bio" tool — it's a full operating system: CRM, payments, invoices, tasks, AI generation, analytics. Linktree only offers a list of links. Taplink is a page builder without CRM or payments.

### Can I connect my own domain?
Yes, on Starter and Pro tiers. Through Cloudflare DNS in "DNS Only" mode (gray cloud) — otherwise CORS errors occur.

### Which payment gateways are supported?
Robokassa (primary for RU/KZ markets), Kaspi Pay (Kazakhstan), Stripe and crypto coming soon.

### How does LinkMAX optimize content for AI?
Automatically generates: FAQPage Schema, Speakable Schema (for voice search), answer blocks (direct answers to questions), LocalBusiness markup. Detects ChatGPT/Perplexity visits and serves them enriched content.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. Backend on Supabase with Row-Level Security. All data isolated by user_id. RLS policies audited by third-party security review.

### Can I collaborate with a team?
Yes, via Business Zones. Invite by email, assign roles (admin/member), everyone sees the shared CRM, inbox, and tasks.

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes: iOS, Android (via Capacitor), and Telegram Mini App.

### Does LinkMAX support English?
Yes. Supported languages: English, Russian, Kazakh, Uzbek. Base fallback is Russian.

### How fast can I get started?
Google sign-up → 30 seconds. AI creates your first page in another 2 minutes. Total: 5 minutes to sales-ready.
